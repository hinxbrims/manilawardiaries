 September 14, 1942

"German Forces enter City of Stalingrad ... street fighting on." Remember Alexandria? Boy, I feel better already. Of course there's nothing in the text to support the news.

A long article on Hongkong admits the Japanese had been advancing to the Sino-British line for several nights prior to the 0400 December 8 opening of hostilities. Kurusu's mission to the States was just a blind!

Father Theo is leaving tomorrow with 15 notes, P500 and plenty of small packages. He tells me the Japanese sent Wainwright, Parker and all high-ranking American Officers to Formosa.
