 September 23, 1942

"Aquino Advises Filipinos to Rely on Their Own Filipinos" — a strong Aquino-type anti-American speech that surprised no one.

Attended a 1-1/2 hour conference with Bishop Binsted. Hellen, Long Ortigas, Castelyn and I were present. We did okay too, procuring 26 out of 29 items wanted. Dr. Waterous will be surprised at the quantity of medicines — if he gets them.
