 November 25, 1942

News roundup: Nothing from the Germans as the Russians make gains in Stalingrad. In Tunisia, as expected, the fighting has bogged down after the Germans regrouped and reinforced. Meanwhile, the Japanese are getting plastered in Buna.

Unintended double entendres: "Our Tomorrow" column: "It is obvious that outside of the marauding guerrillas who are hiding in the mountains and jungles, none of us can afford to be non-collaborationists openly." From the Tribune's "Splashes" column: "Now that the Americans are kicked out, why not do the same to those who want to imitate America?"
