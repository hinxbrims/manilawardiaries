 November 9, 1942

Someone told me that Joe is leaving for Davao tomorrow. All efforts to meet or get packages to him have failed. Even Father Theo couldn't get to first base.

U.S. landings in Africa continue; Algiers has capitulated but Oran is resisting. On the other side of Northern Africa, British armored columns are pursuing Rommel into Libya.
