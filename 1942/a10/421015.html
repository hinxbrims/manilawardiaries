 October 15, 1942

Have I mentioned that the papers have continued to talk about "decisive" battles in Stalingrad? Well, here's some good news: Radio Vichy has reported that snow has begun to fall in northern and central Russia. It's going to be a miserable winter for the Germans.

Hori is amused by the belated admission of U.S. losses. "Evacuation of Solomons by U.S. Expected." Well, I'd expect him to say that.
