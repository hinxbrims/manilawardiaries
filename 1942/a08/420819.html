 August 19, 1942

Tribune: "U.S. hints heavy loss in Solomon Battle," says the Tribune, but Washington is still silent.

Pedaled to town and back three times racing against a coming thunderstorm, all to help Mrs. Menzi distribute Aspirin. Her son, Hans, is still in Santo Tomas on a military matter.
