 June 20, 1942

A new station named "Juan de la Cruz" [the Filipino equivalent of John Doe] is broadcasting thrice daily at 0700, 1300 and 2000. The speaker addresses Tojo and Homma in the most deprecating manner. A sample:

And you, Homma, you son of a bitch, what are you doing in the Philippines? Just sitting around and making your ass wider? You know you won't last much longer, don't you? So stop robbing things.

It's been a long time since I've ridden in a car, streetcar or carretela. I did all three today to get to Harrison Park and back to play softball for the Squires Bingham team. Going down Dewey, we passed a parked truck carrying the burnt out skeleton of the front end of a dive-bomber.
