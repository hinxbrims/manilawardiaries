 June 12, 1944

The Awad bodegas, sealed by PRIMCO for many months, got another sealing last week. The Japanese military sealed them right away from Primco. When informed, Primco could only say: "What can we do?" So the dear public has lost out again. The twenty "honest" retailers are meanwhile still waiting for goods, and so is the public.
