Tribune: "Nippon Forces Occupy Nanning.... Last American Base in Southwest China Now in Japanese Hands." An embarrassed Chungking had nothing to say today.

Machine gunning, Leyte

"Fierce Fighting Rages in Northern Sectors of Leyte." Japanese planes attacked shipping in Leyte Gulf; the box score is now up to 400 vessels exactly! Page 2 details how the "death defying members of the anti-tank corps" destroy American tanks by hurling explosive charges at "vital parts."

P-47s and Warhawks attacked a Japanese convoy bound for Leyte yesterday, sending 3 transports, an escort destroyer, and 3,500 Japanese to the bottom of the sea.
