 April 23, 1944

Tribune: "Premier Tojo Hails P.I. Collaboration, Lauds Progress Here." Said Aquino: "East Asians will win war in the long run." Yes, but under the Allies.

No changes in the reports on Burma — just the same old, dated lies.

Another candidate for one of the BEST war stories:

Heroic feat — from Japanese base in South Pacific, April 20, Domei. It was disclosed that a pilot of one of our planes escorting our convoy crash-dived into a torpedo fired by an enemy submarine and thereby, at the sacrifice of his life, saved the lives of our troops aboard the transport at which the torpedo was fired.
