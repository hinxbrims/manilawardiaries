 July 31, 1943

Tribune: "Inspection Party return impressed by Japan's might.... Members eager to impart knowledge of Japan's strength." The boys are glad and lucky to be back in one piece, and so are the Japanese.

Splashes: "By their safe return, the members of the Inspection Party to Nippon proved once more that the rumor-mongers are all wrong."

Maharajah: "The 17 members ... arrived in Manila yesterday, leaving their doubles in the bottom of the sea where their well-wishers had intended them to remain."
