Vargas' shadow-man, Consul Hirota, stands on his left. Vargas and the Consul look like they're about ready to cry. Kuroda looks wisely at the Consul. The Director General looks like a mad bull. Maurice's party had finished their meal before Vargas was even served. At one point, a piqued Vargas clapped loudly for service.
