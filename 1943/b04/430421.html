 April 21, 1943

Tribune: "Peaceful Life Returns to Cebu." Everywhere the Japanese went, "multitudes" waved flags. What the multitudes did before or after is not fit for printing.

Splashes: "If necessity is the mother of invention, it should at least make us self-sufficient in prime commodities." All we need, therefore, is to discover substitutes for lard, soap, sugar and matches.
