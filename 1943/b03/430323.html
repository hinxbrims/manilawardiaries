 March 23, 1943
Lt. Gen. Shizuichi Tanaka

"General Tanaka Encourages Governors." He just concluded a 25-day tour of the Visayas where he visited Cebu, Panay, Negros and Bohol. He does get around.

The latest joke is a cheer for Aquino: Queremos Aquino [We like Aquino]. Only repeat it a few times and it splits into Aqui No Queremos Aquino [here, we don't like Aquino]. I wonder if Aquino knows.
