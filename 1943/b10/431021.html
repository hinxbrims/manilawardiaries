 October 21, 1943
Laurel's Cabinet

Tribune: All about the Alliance and Laurel and Recto's explanations that it is defensive. After delivering his speech yesterday, Recto entered the PGH with a "minor ailment" — perhaps a nervous breakdown?

Laurel's Cabinet: Recto, Foreign Minister; Antonio de las Alas, Finance; Sison, Justice; Alunan, Commerce and Agriculture; and Paredes, Public Works and Communications — none are pro-Japanese.
