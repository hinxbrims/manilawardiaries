Major General Fortich, governor of Bukidnon Province, Mindanao, has five regiments of guerrillas according to the Nippon Times Weekly. The article admitted his men control quite a sector of coastline. A Japanese expedition force that landed there couldn't locate his guerrillas because they split up and took off for the mountains.
